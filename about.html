What is RES?

As an umbrella organization that brings together sustainably-minded students and administrators, the Rice Environmental Society facilitates collaboration among existing groups in order to promote environmentalism in all aspects of student life.

Our official mission

Over time, Rice University has seen an increase in the number and variety of environmental and sustainability-focused groups on campus. As Rice celebrated its centennial anniversary, a document was passed through the Rice Student Association called the 100-Year-Sustainability Plan. To further the efforts set forth in the 100-Year Plan, we believe it is necessary to provide a channel for students to get involved while promoting interactions between the individual groups. We also recognize that sustainable initiatives require additional funding. Therefore, it is our mission to give students, faculty, and administrators a central name and website to find information about the environmental groups on campus and their initiatives. It is also our mission to generate and provide funding for the environmental groups and environmentally minded individuals to draw from in their sustainable efforts.
